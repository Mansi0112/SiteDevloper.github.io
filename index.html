OUR VISION AND MISSION

COFRONTIERUS

WE HELP TO HEAL

Cofrontierus works to bring about change in the lives of children, young people and vulnerable individuals affected by the pandemic . We educate children. We provide healthcare. We give children, women and the aged a safe home. We train young people for a living. Afterwards, we help them find jobs and even start their own businesses.

Our mission is to bring a ray hope to those with none and change the lives of everyone who sustained the noble disease. We work with our staff, donors, volunteers and partners, collaborators. This year, through our work, we had impacted the lives of over 50,000 children, women and men..

We try to come alongside people and interact with our supporters so that they know that we see them, we’re with them, and we’re in this together.

OVERVIEW

The COFRONTIERUS is a grassroot organization that plays a fundamental role in fighting the COVID-19 at local as well as global level . We bring economic and livelihood opportunities for individuals and families in places across the world . We are striving to reconfigure themselves and to push for new initiatives , backing state medical systems all around the world, by creating national emergency funds, partnering with one another to channel donations, providing assistance in nursing homes and other facilities through technical support and medical teams.
