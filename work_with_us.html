Together, we can bring lasting change to communities by tackling some of our world’s greatest problems.
When you donate your birthday, anniversary, marathon or special event to COFRONTIERUS,

you help us transform the future for millions of men, women and children around the world.

We can’t do it alone, so let’s get started!

COFRONTIERUS a global humanitarian organization that brings sustainable solutions to the world’s greatest problems – disasters, extreme poverty, educational problems and unemployment problems .
We partner with locals and community leaders around the world to bring hope, healing and transformation to the most vulnerable.
Together we're creating change that lasts—today, tomorrow and for generations to come.

You can help us expand our reach and multiply our impact around the world when you join our family of over 95,000 volunteers worldwide.
Your time, energy and resources make all the difference to those who are suffering.

You can defend and speak up on behalf of the vulnerable and suffering around our world.

Together, we can intercede on behalf of those who are suffering, in poverty or without protection,

in order to influence those in positions of power who can save lives.

When you give, you’ll transform the lives of millions of suffering men, women and children around the globe.

In a time of such great need, we need you on our team more than ever before. We cannot do this work without you.

Don’t move through life wishing you’d done more for our suffering world.

God designed you to be a light amidst the darkness, extending his love and compassion to the most vulnerable among us.
